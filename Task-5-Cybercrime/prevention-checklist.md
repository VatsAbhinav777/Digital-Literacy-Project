# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Follow these important tips to stay safe while using the internet:

1. Never share your passwords, OTPs or UPI PIN with anyone. No genuine service will ask for this information.  

2. Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money using UPI apps to avoid sending money to the wrong person.  

3. Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; it is only required when sending money.  

4. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts to improve security.  

5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email, banking and social media.  

6. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received through email, SMS or social media.  

7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or making payments, as it may not be secure.  

8. Keep your apps, phone and system updated to protect against security threats.  


## Report Cybercrime

If you face any cybercrime or fraud, report it immediately:

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
