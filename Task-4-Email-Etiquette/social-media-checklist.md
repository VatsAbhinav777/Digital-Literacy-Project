# Responsible Social Media Use as a College Student

## Do’s

1. Use a professional profile picture and write a clear bio about your studies.  
2. Share useful content like your projects, achievements or learning progress.  
3. Think before posting anything and make sure it is appropriate.  
4. Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal information.  
5. Communicate politely and respectfully with others online.  

## Don’ts

1. Do not post offensive, rude or inappropriate content.  
2. Do not share personal details like phone number, address or passwords.  
3. Avoid spreading false or unverified information.  
4. Do not get involved in online arguments or fights.  
5. Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages.
